Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Sage Companion LLC is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and otherwise handle your information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

Account Registration

When you create a Sage Companion account, we collect:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number (optional), and date of birth
  • Family Information: Names and contact information of authorized family members (optional)

Health-Related Data

You may voluntarily provide health-related information, including:

  • Medications: Names, dosages, frequency, and any notes you add about your medications
  • Doctor Visits: Audio recordings and transcriptions of your conversations with healthcare providers
  • Health Notes: Any other health information you choose to share with the app

AI Conversation Data

We collect and store your conversations with Sage Companion, including:

  • Chat Messages: Both text and voice-to-text transcriptions
  • Voice Recordings: Audio files of your voice input for transcription and AI processing

Usage Data

We automatically collect certain information about your interactions with the app:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version
  • Usage Analytics: Features used, time spent in the app, and interaction patterns
  • Log Data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, and timestamp information

Payment Information

Payment processing is handled securely by Stripe. We do not store your credit card details—Stripe handles all payment processing and storage in compliance with PCI-DSS standards.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

Providing Services

  • Creating and maintaining your account
  • Enabling AI-powered companion features and voice interactions
  • Processing medication reminders and sending notifications
  • Recording and storing your doctor visit conversations for your records
  • Managing your calendar and appointment reminders

Family Monitoring & Dashboard

  • Providing family members with authorized access to monitoring features via family code
  • Sending alerts to family members for important events (medication reminders, appointment notifications)
  • Displaying summary information on the family dashboard only to those you authorize

Service Improvement

  • Analyzing usage patterns to improve our AI companion's responses and features
  • Identifying and fixing technical issues
  • Personalizing your experience based on your preferences
  • Conducting research to enhance our service (with appropriate anonymization)

Communication

  • Sending you account-related notifications and updates
  • Responding to your support inquiries
  • Notifying you of important changes to our Privacy Policy or Terms of Service

Legal Compliance

  • Complying with legal obligations and law enforcement requests
  • Protecting against fraud and ensuring account security

Important: We do NOT use your data for marketing purposes, targeted advertising, or any form of commercial exploitation outside of providing and improving our service to you.

3. Information Sharing & Disclosure

We never sell your data to third parties, data brokers, or advertisers. We are committed to your privacy and will never monetize your personal or health information.

Limited Third-Party Sharing

We only share your information with trusted service providers who help us deliver our service:

Service Provider Purpose Data Shared
OpenAI AI Processing & Companion Features Chat messages, voice transcriptions, conversation context
Stripe Payment Processing Name, email, billing information (no credit card details stored by us)
Resend Email Notifications Email address, notification content
Supabase Data Storage All account and user data

Family Members

When you authorize family members using your unique family code, we share limited information with them:

  • Medication reminders and adherence summary
  • Upcoming appointment information
  • Alert notifications for important events
  • General wellness check-in information

You have full control over what information is shared with family members and can revoke access at any time.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, such as in response to a court order, subpoena, or government request. We will make reasonable efforts to notify you of such requests unless legally prohibited.

Business Transfers

If Sage Companion LLC is involved in a merger, acquisition, bankruptcy, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. You will be notified of any such change and any choices you may have regarding your information.

4. Data Storage & Security

Where Your Data Is Stored

Your information is stored securely in Supabase, which uses PostgreSQL databases hosted on US-based servers. Data is encrypted both in transit and at rest.

Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption in Transit: All communications between your device and our servers use HTTPS encryption
  • Encryption at Rest: Sensitive data is encrypted when stored in our database
  • Password Security: Passwords are hashed using secure algorithms and never stored in plain text
  • Access Controls: Access to your data is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • Regular Security Audits: We conduct regular security reviews and vulnerability assessments
  • Multi-Factor Authentication: We support optional multi-factor authentication for account security

Please note: While we implement comprehensive security measures, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we are committed to protecting your information using the best available methods.

5. Data Retention

Active Account Data

We retain your account information and service data while your account is active and your subscription is ongoing. This includes:

  • Account registration information
  • Medication tracking records
  • Doctor visit recordings and transcriptions
  • Calendar and appointment information
  • Conversation history with the AI companion

After Account Deletion

When you delete your account or your subscription ends, we will:

  • Retain your data for 30 days to allow for account recovery
  • Permanently delete all your personal and health data after the 30-day retention period
  • Remove your data from active backups (archived backups may retain data longer for disaster recovery purposes)

Legal & Compliance Retention

We may retain certain information longer if required by law or for legitimate business purposes, such as:

  • Complying with tax and legal obligations
  • Resolving disputes or enforcing agreements
  • Preventing fraud or ensuring account security

Aggregated Data

We may retain anonymized and aggregated data indefinitely for research, service improvement, and statistical purposes.

6. Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

Right to Access

You have the right to request a copy of all your personal information that we hold. To exercise this right, contact us at support@sagecompanion.com.

Right to Correction

You can update, correct, or modify your personal information directly in your account settings. If you need help, our support team is happy to assist.

Right to Delete (Right to be Forgotten)

You have the right to request deletion of your account and all associated data. Upon request:

  • We will immediately deactivate your account
  • Your data will be retained for 30 days in case you want to recover your account
  • After 30 days, all your personal and health data will be permanently deleted

Right to Data Portability

You can request a copy of your data in a structured, commonly-used, and machine-readable format (such as CSV or JSON). Contact us for assistance with data export.

Right to Withdraw Consent

If you initially consented to certain data processing (such as analytics or family member access), you can withdraw that consent at any time by updating your account settings or contacting us.

Right to Opt-Out of Communications

You can opt out of non-essential communications (such as service updates or feature announcements) through your account settings. Note: We will always send critical service notifications.

How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at:

  • Email: support@sagecompanion.com
  • In-App Support: Use the "Help" or "Settings" menu in the Sage Companion app

We will respond to your request within 30 days (or as required by applicable law).

7. Children's Privacy

Sage Companion is not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18.

If we discover that we have collected information from a child under 18, we will immediately delete that information and any associated account. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately at support@sagecompanion.com.

Note: Parents and guardians may set up family member accounts to monitor a loved one's usage, but primary accounts are intended for seniors 18 and older.

8. Cookies & Tracking

What We Use

We use minimal tracking technologies to deliver and improve our service:

Essential Technologies

  • Service Worker: Enables offline functionality so you can access basic app features without an internet connection
  • localStorage: Stores your session information and app preferences locally on your device
  • Session Cookies: Used to maintain your login session during your visit

What We Do NOT Use

  • Third-party tracking cookies
  • Retargeting or behavioral advertising pixels
  • Cross-site tracking technologies
  • Device fingerprinting

Analytics

We use basic, privacy-respecting analytics to understand how our app is used. This data is:

  • Aggregated and anonymized
  • Never linked to personally identifiable information
  • Used only for service improvement and feature development

Your Choices

You can control storage settings in your browser or app preferences. Note that disabling certain technologies may affect app functionality.

9. California (CCPA) & European (GDPR) Privacy Rights

California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights under the CCPA:

  • Right to Know: Request what personal information we collect and how we use it
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of your personal information (with some exceptions)
  • Right to Opt-Out of Sale: We do not sell your personal information, so this right does not apply
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your CCPA rights

General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Union or United Kingdom, you have the following rights under GDPR:

  • Right to Access: Obtain a copy of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Right to Object: Object to certain processing activities
  • Rights Related to Automated Decision-Making: You have rights regarding automated processing of your data

How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any CCPA or GDPR rights, contact us at:

  • Email: support@sagecompanion.com
  • Mail: Sage Companion LLC (address available upon request)

We will respond to verifiable requests within the timeframes specified by applicable law.
