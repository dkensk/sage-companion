🌿 Getting Started with Sage
  1. Open Sage from your home screen or browser
  2. Tap the Talk button and speak naturally — Sage will listen and respond
  3. You can also type your message in the text box
  4. Use the quick action buttons for Medications, Calendar, Doctor Visits, and Reminders
  5. Sage remembers your conversations and learns about you over time
Tip: You can say things like "Remind me to call the dentist tomorrow" or "When is my next doctor appointment?" and Sage will handle it for you.
💬 Frequently Asked Questions
How do I talk to Sage?
Tap the big Tap to Talk button and speak naturally. Sage will listen, think about what you said, and respond with a friendly voice. You can also type in the text box below the talk button if you prefer.
How do I add my medications?
Tap My Medications, then either scan your medication label with the camera button or tap Add Medication Manually. You can set the name, dose, time, and how many times per day you take it.
How do medication reminders work?
After adding your medications, tap the Enable Reminders button on the Medications screen. You'll get a notification on your phone when it's time to take your medicine. You can mark it as taken right from the notification.
How do I add a doctor's appointment?
You can say to Sage: "I have a doctor's appointment on Thursday at 2 PM" and Sage will add it to your calendar. You can also tap My Calendar and add it manually. Sage will remind you 1 hour and 15 minutes before your appointment.
What is the Get Help button for?
The red Get Help button at the top is for emergencies. It can alert your family member and give you the option to call 911. Only use it when you need immediate help.
What is my Family Code?
Your Family Code is a special code that lets your family members connect to your Sage account. Share it with a trusted family member so they can check on your medications, appointments, and get emergency alerts. You can find it in Settings.
Can Sage give me medical advice?
No — Sage is a companion, not a doctor. Sage will never give medical advice, diagnose conditions, or recommend treatments. If you ask a health question, Sage will suggest you talk to your doctor and can even save the question for your next visit.
How do I add Sage to my home screen?
On iPhone: Open Sage in Safari, tap the Share button (the box with an arrow), then tap Add to Home Screen. On Android: Tap the three-dot menu in Chrome, then tap Add to Home Screen. This lets you open Sage like a regular app.
Is my information private?
Yes. Your conversations, medications, and health information are private and encrypted. Only you and your connected family members can see your information. We never sell or share your personal data. See our Privacy Policy for details.
I accidentally marked a medication as taken. Can I undo it?
Yes! On the Medications screen, tap the green Taken badge next to the medication you want to undo. It will be unmarked so you can take it at the right time.
🔧 Troubleshooting
Sage can't hear me when I tap Talk
Make sure your phone's microphone is turned on for your browser. On iPhone, go to Settings → Safari → Microphone and make sure it's allowed. Also check that your phone isn't on silent mode.
I'm not getting medication reminders
Make sure you tapped Enable Reminders on the Medications screen and allowed notifications when your phone asked. On iPhone, check Settings → Notifications → Safari (or your browser) and make sure notifications are turned on.
The app looks old or isn't showing my changes
Try closing the app completely and reopening it. If that doesn't work, clear your browser's saved data for this site. The app updates automatically — sometimes it just needs a fresh restart.
I forgot my password or family code
On the login screen, tap Forgot password? or Forgot family code? to get help recovering your account. If you're still having trouble, contact us and we'll help you get back in.
