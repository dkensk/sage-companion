Get Started

Up and Running in Minutes

Sage is designed to be simple. Here's how to begin your journey.

1
Open Sage
Launch Sage from your home screen or open it in your web browser.
2
Start Talking
Tap the Talk button and speak naturally — Sage will listen and respond with a friendly voice.
3
Type If You Prefer
You can also type your message in the text box at the bottom of the screen.
4
Use Quick Actions
Tap the tiles for Medications, Calendar, Doctor Visits, and Reminders.
5
Sage Learns You
Over time, Sage remembers your conversations and personalizes your experience.
💡
Pro tip: You can say things like "Remind me to call the dentist tomorrow" or "When is my next doctor appointment?" and Sage will handle it for you.

Troubleshooting

Something Not Working?

Here are quick fixes for the most common issues.

🎤
Sage Can't Hear Me
Make sure your phone's microphone is turned on for your browser. On iPhone, go to Settings → Safari → Microphone and make sure it's allowed. Also check that your phone isn't on silent mode.
🔔
No Medication Reminders
Make sure you tapped Enable Reminders on the Medications screen and allowed notifications when your phone asked. Check Settings → Notifications → Safari and make sure they're turned on.
🔄
App Looks Outdated
Try closing the app completely and reopening it. If that doesn't work, clear your browser's saved data for this site. The app updates automatically — sometimes it just needs a fresh restart.
🔑
Forgot Password or Code
On the login screen, tap Forgot password? or Forgot family code? to get help recovering your account. If you're still having trouble, contact us and we'll help you.
